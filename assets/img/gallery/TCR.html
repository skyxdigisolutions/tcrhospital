Welcome To TCR Multispeciality Hospital

TCR Multispeciality Hospital, a 100 bedded Multispeciality hospital located in Krishnagiri ,Tamilnadu, India known for its standards of excellence in management of critically ill patients . We are specialized in Departments of Critical Care, Poison Care, Diabetology, Cardiology, Nephrology, Neurology, Advanced Clinical Dentistry, ENT, Orthopedics, Gynecology, Plastic Surgery, Anesthesiology and General Surgery.

TCR Multispeciality Hospitalwas established by Dr. C. SOUNDARA RAJ, M.D., (General Medicine), Fellowship in Cardiology at Madras Medical Mission, Chennai. The Hospital was established in the year 2001 with the prime objective of providing the best healthcare services to SNAKE BITES & POISONING across South India and beyond. The hospital has set a new benchmark of excellence in medical care with ultramodern and state-of-the-art facilities. It is a 24-hour Multispeciality Hospital. Read More
